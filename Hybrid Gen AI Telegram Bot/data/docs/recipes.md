# Team Lunch Recipes

## Pasta Salad
Boil pasta, cool it, and mix with cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, feta, olive oil, and lemon juice. Chill for at least thirty minutes before serving.

## Veggie Wraps
Fill tortillas with hummus, grilled vegetables, lettuce, and a yogurt-herb dressing. Slice into halves for easy serving.

## Brownies
Combine butter, sugar, eggs, cocoa powder, flour, and vanilla. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 25 minutes.
