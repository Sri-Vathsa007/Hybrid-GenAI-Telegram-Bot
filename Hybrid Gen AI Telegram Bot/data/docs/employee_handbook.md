# Employee Handbook Quick Guide

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time.

## Leave Policy
Paid time off requests should be submitted at least five business days in advance. Emergency leave must be communicated to the manager and HR as soon as possible.

## Security Rules
Never share passwords over email or chat. Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all company systems. Lost devices must be reported within one hour.
