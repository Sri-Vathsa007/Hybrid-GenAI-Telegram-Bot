# IT Support FAQ

## How do I reset MFA?
Go to the identity portal and choose "Reset verification method." If you cannot access the portal, contact the IT helpdesk and request a temporary bypass code. Identity verification requires your employee ID and manager confirmation.

## Laptop Replacement
If a laptop is damaged, open a support ticket with the device serial number, issue summary, and a photo if physical damage is visible. Standard replacement SLA is two business days.

## VPN Access
VPN is required for production dashboards and internal admin tooling. Use the corporate VPN client and authenticate using MFA.
