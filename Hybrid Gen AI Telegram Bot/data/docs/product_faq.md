# Product FAQ

## What does the analytics dashboard show?
The analytics dashboard displays daily active users, conversion funnel performance, revenue trends, and operational incidents. Product managers use it to spot anomalies and measure release impact.

## How often is data refreshed?
Operational metrics refresh every fifteen minutes. Financial reports refresh nightly at 2:00 AM UTC.

## Who owns incident review?
The reliability engineering manager facilitates the incident review, while the feature team prepares the timeline and remediation plan.
