# On-Call Runbook

## Severity Levels
Sev-1 incidents block critical workflows or revenue paths and require immediate escalation to engineering leadership. Sev-2 incidents degrade a major feature but have a workaround.

## First Response
Acknowledge the alert within five minutes, assign an incident commander, and start a dedicated incident channel. Update the status page within fifteen minutes for customer-facing impact.

## After Action
Document the root cause, customer impact, timeline, and remediation items within two business days.
